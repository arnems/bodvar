EX-01  
STEINENE SYNGER / THE STONES SING / ES SINGEN DIE STEINE / LE CHANT DES PIERRES

BODVAR SCHJELDERUP / FREDAG / 1989   

Travelling exhibition.  56 colour charts 60x60 cm (24 x 24 in),
plus hinged upper and lower parts for titles and text, respectively (total unfolded size 60x120 cm (24x48 in).
Transportation in a single wooden container; gross weight 390 kg.
To be mounted on tables; minimum width 130 cm (or double table width 2 x 65 cm – see photographs).
Texts in Norwegian, English, German, and French.  Supplementary pamphlet texts in Swedish, Hungarian, Russian, Estonian, Latvian.

Topics :
 
KEY EXAMPLES OF SACRED GEOMETRY & CODES;
THE GREAT PYRAMID AND ITS EXPLAINING SYMBOLS & GEOMETRICAL CODES;  THE KEY OF TRANSITION AS REPRESENTED IN SACRED ARCHITECTURE;
THE HEBREW EXODUS AND ITS CONNECTION WITH THE GREAT PYRAMID;
YESHUAN PLACES IN PALESTINE AND CONNECTIONS WITH THE GREAT PYRAMID;
SACRED JERUSALEM: TEMPLE MOUNT ANALYSIS; YESHUAN KEY OF TRANSITION;
A PARABLE OF LEVELS: GREAT PYRAMID INTERIOR & SACRED TOPOGRAPHY;

Presentations inNorway: Trondheim (St Olav Festival); Oslo (Oslo Rådhus)
Presentations abroad: The Netherlands (Eindhoven Techn. University);
Hungary (Budapest - National Culture Festival – and Pecs (TechnicalCollege);
Germany (Düsseldorf/Neuss – World Congress);  Estonia (Tallinn, and Tartu);
Russia (Moscow – congress); 
(Do require this exhibit for presentation in your own environment!)

BODVAR SCHJELDERUP / PERISCOPE VISION / 2005…
