images of ascent

The term ‘ascent’ refers to the Great Pyramid passages, characterized by a gradient angle of 26.28 deg = 2628 ac. A variety of appearances outside the Pyramid makes the term explain the phenomenon in general.

temaet ‘oppstigning’

‘Oppstigning’ refererer til Pyramidens ganger, med en stigning på 2628 ac. En rekke eksempler utenfor Pyramiden har tilsvarende symbolske meldinger.
