‘sacred geometry’

The wise of old saw that number and geometry are sources to insight, more pure and reliable than truths mediated by words. The ageless capacities have always been a guide to searching minds, and will still be just that ...

‘hellig geometri’

De gamle vise innså at tall og geometri ga innsikt som ordene ikke evnet å formidle. Det tidløse språket har til alle tider bevist sine avslørende egenskaper.
